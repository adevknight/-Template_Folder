    ... "none"
      To reset the transform object to original shape, position, and size.

    ... "matrix"
      It is best to use multiple transform rather than this value.
      E.g : transform: translate(10px, 30px) rotate(30deg) scale(2, 1.5) skewX(23deg).
    
    ... "scaleY(-1)""
      To reflect it by horizontal line or to reflect it vertically for scale of 1.
